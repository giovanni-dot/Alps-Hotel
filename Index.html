Our Heritage

For over a century, Alpine Grand has welcomed discerning travelers to experience Swiss hospitality at its finest.

Established in 1912, Alpine Grand has stood as a beacon of luxury amidst the breathtaking Swiss Alps. Our heritage is built upon a foundation of exceptional service, timeless elegance, and deep respect for the majestic natural surroundings.

Each detail of our hotel reflects the perfect harmony between Alpine tradition and contemporary luxury. From our handcrafted furniture to our locally-sourced gourmet cuisine, we celebrate the rich culture and natural beauty of Switzerland.

Our dedicated staff, many of whom come from generations of hoteliers, are committed to creating unforgettable experiences for our guests, whether you're seeking adventure on the slopes or tranquility in our world-class spa.
